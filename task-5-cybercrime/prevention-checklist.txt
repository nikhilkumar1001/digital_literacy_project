Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

-   Always verify the sender of emails and messages before clicking on
    any links.
-   Check website URLs carefully and ensure they start with “https://”
    before entering sensitive information.
-   Never share OTPs, passwords, or bank details with anyone, even if
    the request appears genuine.
-   Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts to prevent
    unauthorized access.
-   Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for additional security on
    important accounts.
-   Avoid using public Wi-Fi for online banking or financial
    transactions.
-   Regularly update your apps, software, and antivirus to protect
    against security threats.
-   Be cautious of messages that create urgency or fear, such as
    “account will be blocked immediately.”
-   Monitor your bank and UPI transactions regularly to detect any
    unauthorized activity.
